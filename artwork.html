Artwork

Title: FISHES IN THE SKY
Medium: Colour pencil on paper
Size: 6”x6”
My deep sea diving experience propelled me to design this artwork. I was inspired by the bright sky and the vivid colours and patterns of marine life in the ocean. Combining these two elements together, I created repeated digitised patterns depicting the vibrant “Fishes in the Sky”



Title: LOOKING BEYOND
Medium: Newspaper Cutting on Paper
Size: 9” x 11”
This particular artwork depicts a woman’s eye narrating stories of pain as well as beauty.
A simple newspaper collage-making technique learnt during childhood served as an inspiration. It reflects how tiny bits of inconsequential confetti can form meaningful artwork depicting innate musings. This taught me how simple tools can transform art into a powerful creation.



Title: CERISE
Medium: Acrylic on Canvas
Size: 18” x 18”
It’s like I took all my emotions, the good feelings, the bad feelings and put it all into something which is passionate and artistic. It’s raw, it’s honest, it’s incomplete but it’s me. It’s all my emotions spilled out on a blank canvas.




Title: TRANSLATION OF TUNES
Medium: Pen on paper
Size: 16.5” x 11.5”
I created a hand drawn ink illustration of a cityscape in two point perspective. Trying to discover new ways of exploring perspective through architecture, I have created an illusion of space on a flat surface.


Title: BOOZO
Medium: Charcoal and Eraser on Paper
Size: 16.5” x 11.5”
My white labrador, Boozo was the inspiration behind this. The portrait reflects a sense of playfulness, loyalty and faithfulness, all of which remind me of how my dog was. I used the technique of reverse charcoal drawing to create this artwork.


Title: UNDRESS MY MIND
Medium: Acrylic on Canvas
Size: 18”x24”
Anyone can undress your body but it takes alot to undress your mind. It’s beautiful when you find someone that is in love with your mind. Someone that wants to watch you slowly take down all the walls you’ve built up around your mind and let them inside all your thoughts and insecurities.
