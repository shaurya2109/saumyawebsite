Who Am I?

A relief structure which depicts my personality through the use of visual metaphors.

This relief structure truly depicts my innate self in all aspects. It has been constructed keeping in mind my attributes at different times and around different people. The first pillar on the left depicts who I am when I am around people that I like; I am similar to a branch of the same tree, positive like a lotus in muddy water and strong headed about my thoughts and opinions. The second pillar highlights my two most significant qualities which are reflective and enthusiastic. The compositions fixed on the ground depict me at my best: mouldable like clay and comforting like McDonaldâ€™s in a health food market; and at my worst: extremely silent and underestimated. The one raised on the right most corner depicts one of my very distinct qualities of being easily understandable by both people close to me as well as strangers.
